Welcome to TechTycoon

An Educational Business Simulation Game

In TechTycoon, you'll lead a technical team through real-world challenges faced by tech companies serving students and schools.

You'll make critical decisions about how to:

  • Solve technical problems
  • Manage limited budgets
  • Balance team needs with company reputation
  • Prioritize impact and inclusivity

Your choices will determine your scores across four key metrics: Impact, Inclusivity, Trust, and Budget. Can you solve the crisis while keeping everyone satisfied?
